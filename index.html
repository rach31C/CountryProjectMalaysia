    • ABOUT MALAYSIA

    Malaysia features a large range of Asian cultures. From West Asia to East Asia, Malaysia is home to a diverse population. Here are some key facts about Malaysia!

  • Population: 33.57 million
  • Government: Federal constitutional monarchy
  • Currency: Ringgit (RM)
  • Capital: Kuala Lumpur
  • Fun Fact: The Malayisan flag has 14 stripes to represent their 13 states and 1 federal territory!
