Kelantan

Fun Fact: Kelantan translates to "Land of Lightning"!

KELANTAN ATTRACTIONS

Siti Khadijah Market

Siti Khadijah Market is a visual feast where one can find various of edible goods. From local snacks and pickles to fresh vegetables and spices, Kelantanis known for its assortment of desserts!


Gunung Stong

Gunung Stong is Malaysia's most popular spots for eco-adventure. Its great seven-tiered Jelawang Waterfall is said to be the highest in Southeast Asia. Gunung Stong is also home to a variety of animals and plant species!


Caving in Gua Ikan and Gua Gelap

To access the caves, people must go through Dabong. One of the caves "Gua Ikan" translates to "fish cave" while the other, "Gua Gelap", translates to "dark cave". Gua Gelap lives up to its name as suggested as there are small crevices on the cave walls being the only source of natural light. Other caves such as Gua Cha, Gua Pintu and Gua Sidang as worth going to as well!
