Kedah

Fun Fact: Kedah is most famous for its island, Langkawi!

KEDAH ATTRACTIONS

Dataran Lang

Dataran Lang, also known as the, "Eagle Square", is surrounded with beautiful scenery and local stalls that sell traditonal pottery and handicrafts!


Underwater World Langkawi

Underwater World Langkawi is the biggest aquarium in Malaysia! It is home to over 40,000 species of marine life spanning over 60,000 feet. It also includes a 3D theatre, Educational Resource Centre and a cafeteria!


Zahir Mosque

The Zahir Mosque is one of the oldest and grandest mosques in Malaysia. Throughout the entire world, it is considered as one of the 10 most beautiful mosques!
