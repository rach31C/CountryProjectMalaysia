PERAK ATTRACTIONS

Zoo Taiping & Night Safari

Zoo Taiping is Malaysia's first zoo! It is home to 180 local and exotic wildlife species. The Night Safari allows visitors to ride on a mini train around the zoo to see the zoo's nocturnal animals!


Felda Residence Hot Springs

The Felda Residence Hot Springs is filled with natural, free-flowing hot springs. These hot springs are said to have healing properties for aliments such as arthritis and eczema. It is very relaxing and peaceful and greatly recommended for elderlies and weak people.


Teluk Intan Leaning Tower

At a height of 25.5 meters (80-stories tall) the Teluk Intan used to be a straight tower that held water during dry seasons. However, it began to tilt to the left in 1889, four years after it was initally built. Today, it is served as a special landmark!
