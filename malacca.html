Malacca

Fun Fact: Malacca is home to the most influential port and first great maritime Kingdom in the Malay Archipelago!

MALACCA/MELAKA ATTRACTIONS

Melaka Tropical Fruit Farm

Melaka Tropical Fruit Farm holds many fresh tropical fuits such as the durian, rambutan and mangosteen! Over 173 acres of land, the farm comprises an orchard, herb gande, exotic plants and more. They also have mini zoos with various animals such as camels and ostriches. Many activites can be done there which include kayaking, animal feeding, jungle-trekking, etc.


Taming Sari Tower

Enjoy the 360 degree view of Banda Hiliar at the Taming Sari Tower! The tower is dedicated to the Taming Sari dagger. This dagger is a legendary weapon welded by the Malay warror, Hang Tuah. Attractions also include the Mini Rider 3D, Pony Ride and Italian Bumper Car.


A'Famosa Fort (Porta de Santiago)

Porta de Santiago is one of Malacca's most famous landmarks! It is one of the four main entraces to the A'Famousa fortress, which was built in 1511 by a Portuguese admiral. Although most parts of the fortress were badly damanged during the Dutch invasion in 1641, it still stands tall to this day!
