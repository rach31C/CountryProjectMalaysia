Selangor

Fun Fact: Selangor is one of the most developed states in Malaysia!

SELANGOR ATTRACTIONS

Sky Mirror

Named after the largest salt flat in Bolivia (known as the World's Largest Natural Mirror), visitors can see the same thing in Malaysia at the Sasaran Beach. It is an expansive stretch of mirror-like sea that seems to be one with the sky, giving it a great backdrop effect! It is great for people who like to take photos!


Kuala Kubu Bharu

Only about 50 kilometers from Kuala Lumpur, visitors can come to raft for fun or for a challenge! Kuala Kubu Bharu also includes the Sungai Chilling Waterfalls and hiking at Buky Kutu.


Sunway Lagoon

Sun Lagoon is perfect to go with family and friends as it suits every age group! Sun Lagoon offers 5 different theme parks in just one location, featuring over 80 rides and attractions!
