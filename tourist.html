Tourist Attractions

Malaysia has many unique and fun places to go! Sorted based on popularity and state!

POPULAR DESTINATIONS

Kuala Lumpur, Federal Territory

Kuala Lumpa is best known for it's twin towers, the Petrona Towers. In fact, it used to hold the record of being the tallest tower in the world! However, it was overtaken by Taipei 101 in 2001. Regardless, the Petrona Towers is now considered the tallest twin towers in the world! It is very tourist friendly and has many flashy buildings!


Penang Street Art, Penang

Penang Street Art is one of the most notable places in Penang. Featuring over 50+ street arts throughout Georgetown, they all have a small backstory behind it showcasing the culture in Penang! The phot on the right, is by far the most famous art piece of all, called "Little Children on a Bicycle" by Ernest Zacharevic.


Batu Caves, Selangor

Batu is a malay word for "rock", making the name literally mean "Rock Caves". Batu caves attracts many Hindu worshippers and tourists alike, especially during Thaipusam, a Hindu festival. The golden statue infront of the cave is Lord Murugan, the god of war.


Langkawi Sky Bridge, Kedah

The Langkawi Sky Bridge is one of the world's longest curved suspension bridge! The view from the bridge is breathtaking! To get to the bridge, people must ride a 15 minute cable car ride.


LEGOLAND Malaysia, Johor

Being the very first international theme park in Malaysia, LEGOLAND Malaysia offers many fun rides and games for families to enjoy! In fact, it is home to the first LEGO-themed Virtual Reality Roller Coaster! LEGOLAND Malaysia is great for people who love LEGOS and kids!
