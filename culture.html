Malaysian Culture

Malaysia has an extremely diverse culture ranging from every place in Asia!

CULTURE

The People

The Malaysian population largely consists of Native Malays, Chinese and Indian people! This means that they are a multi-cultural society. As shown in the left, many of the people continue to wear their own traditional clothing.


The Dances

Dancing is a very important part of Malaysian Culture. It expresses their dedication to their religion. Plus, they are often performed during festivals and celebrations.


The Religions

Malaysia is home to many different religions including, Islam, Buddhism, Christianity, and Hinduism. These percentages are as follows: 63.5% of the population practices Islam, 18.7% Buddhism, 9.1% Christianity, 6.1% Hinduism and 2.7% as other religion or that no information has been given.
