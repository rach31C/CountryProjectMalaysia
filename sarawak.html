Sarawak

Fun Fact: Sarawak is the biggest state in Malaysia!

SARAWAK ATTRACTIONS

Gunung Mulu National Park

The Gunung Mulu National Park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It has many complex cave networks and rare geological formations. It is also home to thousands of animals and plant species and the world's largest cave passage, and natural cave chamber! It is also said that there is a geological formation that resembles the side profile of Abraham Lincoln!


Niah National Park

Adventure into the Niah National Park where visitors will learn about the preshistoric men who lived inside the cave nearly 40,000 years ago. It's a great place for history lovers and researchers to learn about the early humans!


Cat Museum

Cat Museum is a great place for many cat-lovers out there! It features all kinds of feline depiction in all different art mediums such as statues, paintings and etc.
