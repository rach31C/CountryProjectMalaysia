Fun Fact: Negri Sembilan literally means "9 states" as it used to consist of 9 districts!

NEGERI SEMBILAN ATTRACTIONS

Alive 3D Art Gallery

This gallery contains over 50 pains and optical illusions with 3D art. They feature 5 fives: adventure, aquarium, animation, exotic and dark art. Vistiors are able to interact and blend in with the art pieces! (An example is shown to the right)


Army Museum, Port Dickson

Home to the first Malay Regiment batch in 1933, the Army Museum showcases the history, contributions and achievements of the Malaysian army. They showcase canons, tanks, locomotives and other equipment used during to war. They both fully inform and simulate what the soliders felt and used during wartime.


Ulu Bendul Recreational Forest

The Ulu Bendul Recreational Forest is located within the Gunung Angsi Forest Reserve. This place is perfect for outdoor activities such as hiking or camping out. Devoted climbers as climb as high as 825 meters in Gunung Angsi.
