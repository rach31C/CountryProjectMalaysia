Sabah

Fun Fact: Sabah shares the second oldest rainforest in the world, Borneo Rainforest, with Brunei and Indonesia.

SABAH ATTRACTIONS

Mount Kinabalu

Mount Kinabalu is Malaysia's first UNESCO World Heritage Site. It is one of the highest peaks throughout Southeast Asia. It towers the centrepiece of Kinabalu Park, standing at 4,095.2m above sea level! Mount Kinabalu is full od ecological treasures!


Sepilok Orang Utan Rehabilitation Centre

Enjoy the opportunity to see an orangatang up close! This rehabilitation centre gives orphaned orangatangs care and love that they need. They also educate the public on the importance of wildlife conservation!


Upside Down House

The Upside Down House in Sabah is one of the only 5 upside down structures in the world! The house is literally upside down. The floor on the ceiling while the ceiling on the floor.
