TERENGGANU ATTRACTIONS

Pulau Kapas and Pulau Gemia

This place is a small paradise which homes both marine life and diverse corals! The island is known for squid fishing during specific ties of the year. It an amazing place for people who want to escape the city life and enjoy themselves in activities such as scuba diving, and jungle trekking.


Bukit Maras Paragliding & Recreational Park

Bukit Maras is a great place to go to overlook the state's beauty. From their airports to their rivers and orchards, the hilly terrain allows visitors to do many activiies such as hiking, paragliding, etc.


Pantai Teluk Mak Nik

Pantai Teluk Mak Nik is sometimes referred to as Monica Bay. It is an important nesting group for turtles! It is a turtle sanctuary where visitors can learn more about conservation efforts and view newborn hatchlings. In fact, they allow the visitors to help release the turtle hatchlings into the wild, further promoting their conservation efforts.
