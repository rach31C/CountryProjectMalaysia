Malaysia History & Culture

Learn about Malaysia's History and Culture! Find more below by exploring these topics seperately!

History

Malaysia history is no ordinary one. Their history spans over 500 years! From their famous trading ports to European Colonialism to today's rich culture, the country is packed with interesting backstories!

Culture

The culture in Malaysia is not only fascinating but very unique! Due to their history of trading, they were able to have many merchants and people settle in the country to share their culture and religion!
